◆ FibrosisState

enum class hepce::data::FibrosisState
strong

HCV infection causes liver fibrosis and cirrhosis and increases the risk of development of hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC). These states strictly increase, with the possibility of progressing to HCC being possible at any time from stage F3 and higher.

Enumerator
kF0 
kF1 

No Scarring.

kF2 

Mild Liver Scarring.

kF3 

Scarring has occurred and extends outside the liver area.

kF4 

Fibrosis spreading and forming bridges with other fibrotic liver areas.

kDecomp 

Cirrhosis or advanced scarring.

kNone 

Symptomatic cirrhosis; over complications.

kCount 

No adverse liver effects.
